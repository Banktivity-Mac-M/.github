## 🖌 Overview  
Banktivity Advanced is a comprehensive personal finance management application that consolidates account tracking, budget management, investment monitoring, and financial goal planning into a single organized environment for complete visibility over your financial life.

Direct bank connection synchronizes transaction data from linked financial institutions automatically, keeping your account balances and transaction history current without manual data entry for each transaction.

Budget tracking compares actual spending against configured budgets in real time, giving you immediate feedback on how your current month is progressing relative to your financial plan before the month ends.

Investment portfolio monitoring displays holdings, performance, allocation, and gain/loss data across all connected investment accounts, providing a consolidated view of your investment position without navigating to each brokerage account separately.

For individuals who want complete, organized visibility into every aspect of their financial situation, Banktivity Advanced is the personal finance platform that brings all your accounts, spending, and investments together in one clear, actionable view.
